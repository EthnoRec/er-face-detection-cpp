\subsection{Detailed Description}
box with integer boundaries 

Boxes are defined as tuples (x1, y1, x2, y2), where x1$<$=x2, y1$<$=y2; so (x1,y1) is the top-\/left corner, (x2,y2) is the bottom-\/right corner.
